Who We Are

We are a group of solution driven individuals passionate about creating environmentally friendly opportunities for women in Africa.

Our aim is to supply solar powered refrigerator to women in the fisheries supply chain through either direct sale, individual lease or communal lease. These efforts will enable women fish retail traders be more profitable while repaying the lease over an agreed term. We intend to invest 5% of our profit into R&D works to clean our waters which links to the Atlantic Ocean.

Individual Lease

We lease our product to individual fish retailing SME’s who repay us for the asset for an agreed contract term. For more information please contact us.

Communal Lease

We rent out space to individual fish retailing SME’s who can’t afford to own their asset for an agreed contract term. For more information please contact us.
