Who We Are

We are a group of solution driven individuals passionate about creating environmentally friendly opportunities for women in Africa.

Eja-Ice offers solar powered refrigerator to women in the fisheries supply chain through either direct sale, individual or communal Asset Credit Sale. These efforts enable women fish retail traders to be more profitable, adopting cheaper and tailor suited repayment plan over an agreed term. As part of our corporate social responsibilities, we invest 5% of our profit into R&D works to clean our waters which links to the Atlantic Ocean.

Personal Asset Credit Sales

We offer our products to individual fish retailers who pay for the asset over an agreed contract term. For more information please contact us.

Joint Asset Credit Sales

We offer our products to groups of fish retailers who co-own the asset and pay for the asset over an agreed contract term. For more information please contact us.
